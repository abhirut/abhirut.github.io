Research Interest

My research interest lies in exploring application of machine learning techniques to open problems in Natural Language Processing. Areas of interest include Natural Language Processing, Information Extraction, Information Retrieval, and Machine Learning.

In particular, I would like to explore techniques for understanding unstructured information like language or images with limited supervision.
